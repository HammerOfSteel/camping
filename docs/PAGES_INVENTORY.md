### 4. CANOEING PAGE `/canoeing`

#### Header/Hero
- **Title**: "Canoeing"
- **Subheading**: Descriptive text about Nordic wilderness adventure

#### Main Content

**Introduction Text**:
```
"Here in Jämtland, you can count on an unforgettable experience in untouched nature.
We can offer you a tailor-made canoe trip of several rivers that are far from the big city hustle and bustle."
```

**Wildlife & Nature**:
- 30,000 wild moose in Jämtland (1 per square km)
- Also: bears, beavers, reindeer
- Authentic tranquility and peace
- Possible to avoid crowds

**Fishing**:
- Very good fishing opportunities
- Target species: grayling, char, trout
- Fishing license required (can purchase on ifiske.se for ~500 SEK/week, separate Norwegian license for Norway side)

**Canoe Center Overview**:
- One of Norrland's largest canoe centers
- Offers day trips, weekend trips, 1-2 week trips
- Transport provided to appropriate starting place in wilderness
- Can start from campsite for day trips
- Nice to canoe in spring/autumn as well as summer

**Key Links**:
- "To read more about our tours, click here!" → `/harkantrip`
- "Prices here!" → `/pricescanoeing`

**Photo Gallery Section**:

### 9. PRICES - CANOEING PAGE `/pricescanoeing`

#### Pricing Details Listed
- **Day trip (max 5 hours)**: 200 SEK/person
- **Longer day trip (5+ hours)**: 300 SEK/person
- **Camping guest discount**: 50% on day trips
- **Kids**: Free when accompanied by adult family member

**Longer Trips**:
- Mångbodarna day trip (16-20 km): 500 SEK/person (Min. 2,000 SEK)
- 40 km 2-day trip: 1,200 SEK/person (Min. 3,500 SEK)
- 50-60 km 3-day trip: 1,800 SEK/person (Min. 4,200 SEK)
- 90 km ~5 day trip: 2,500 SEK/person (Min. 5,500 SEK)
- 110 km ~7 day trip: 3,200 SEK/person
- 150 km ~10 day trip: 3,500 SEK/person

**Special Notes**:
- All prices per adult
- Family discounts available (varies, contact for quote)
- School/large group custom pricing available

### 10. HÅRKAN TRIP PAGE `/harkantrip`

#### Header
- **Title**: "Harkantrip"

#### Introduction
```
"The 'Hårkan-trip' is our most popular route. Partially because the water levels 
in the Hårkan river are almost always high enough for canoeing. Passing through 
the swedish fjäll, lakes and forests makes for an unforgettable nordic experience."
```

**Why Hårkan?**: Reliable water levels year-round (vs. Storån and Långan)

#### Six Route Sections (Detailed)

Each route includes:
- Starting location name
- Total distance (km)
- Estimated duration
- Detailed description of journey, hazards, alternative routes
- Camping spots, fishing info, wildlife info, landmarks
- Special notes and safety warnings
- Maps/images where applicable

**Routes Listed**:

1. **Ulen-Lake (Norway) 150 km (10 days)**
2. **Valsjö-Village 110 km (7 days)**
3. **Ålviken 90 km (5 days)**
4. **Lövsjön-Lake 60 km (3 days)**
5. **Näsaforsen-Stream 50 km (2-3 days)**
6. **Edsforsen-Stream 40 km (2 days)** - "Suitable for almost everyone"

**Key Features Across Routes**:
- Multiple wind shelters (15+ on lower sections)
- Camping sites approximately every 1-2 km
- Fishing opportunities with license
- Wildlife viewing potential
- Detailed hazard navigation guidance
- Maps showing bridges, dams, outlets

**Images**: kanotgalleri images showing route maps and scenic views

**Closing**:
```
"Don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions regarding any stretch of the Hårkan route."
```
