## Hårkan River Routes (Most Popular)

The Hårkan river has the most reliable water levels and is the primary canoe route offered.

### Route Details

| Starting Point | Distance | Typical Duration | Highlights |
|---------------|----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Ulen Lake (Norway) | 150 km | 10 days | Most remote, waterfall, national park, moose & beaver |
| Valsjö Village | 110 km | 7 days | Still remote, fishing, villages |
| Ålviken | 90 km | ~5 days | Peaceful bay start, scenic |
| Lövsjön Lake East | 60 km | 3 days | Moderate trip, good fishing |
| Näsaforsen Stream | 50 km | 2-3 days | Hydro plant starting, good fishing variety |
| Edsforsen Stream | 40 km | 2 days | Shortest, most accessible, 15+ wind shelters to camping |

### What Makes Hårkan Special
- Highly reliable water levels year-round (vs. Storån/Långan which often have low water)
- Passes through Swedish fjäll, lakes, and forests
- Abundant wildlife: 30,000 moose, bears, beavers, reindeer possible sightings
- Excellent fishing: grayling, char, trout, pike, perch
- Wind shelters every 1-2 km on lower portions
- Suitable campsites throughout
