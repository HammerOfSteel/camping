# Design Direction & Branding Guide

## Visual Identity

### Brand Statement
**Lits Camping** is the peaceful Nordic answer to the question: "Where can I truly escape?" A family-run sanctuary where authentic hospitality meets untamed wilderness, and every guest becomes part of a welcoming community.

### Core Values to Convey
1. **Authenticity** - Real family business, genuine warmth
2. **Peace** - Nature immersion, stress relief, reconnection
3. **Adventure** - Wilderness experiences, canoe expeditions
4. **Community** - Belonging, shared experiences, family-friendly
5. **Nordic Heritage** - Scandinavian design, wilderness tradition
6. **Stewardship** - Respect for nature, sustainable practices

## Visual Design System

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Forest Green**: `#2D5016` or `#3A6B35` - Nature, growth, calm
- **Nordic Blue**: `#1B5E7F` or `#2E7FA4` - Water, sky, trust
- **Cream White**: `#F7F5F0` - Clean, spacious, peaceful
- **Warm Brown**: `#6B4423` or `#8B6F47` - Wood, earth, comfort

#### Secondary Colors
- **Sage Green**: `#9CAF88` - Soft nature accent
- **Soft Gold**: `#C9A876` - Warmth, hospitality
- **Deep Charcoal**: `#3A3A3A` - Text, contrast
- **Light Gray**: `#E8E8E8` - Backgrounds, borders

#### Accent Colors
- **Sunrise Orange**: `#E8814F` or `#D97E4A` - Energy, call-to-action, adventure
- **Water Blue**: `#4A9FBE` - Canoeing, activity, fun

### Typography

#### Font Pairings
**Option 1 (Recommended)**: Modern + Warm
- **Headers**: Outfit (Bold, 700-800) or Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body**: Inter (Regular 400, Medium 500)
- **Accent**: Playfair Display (for emotional moments)

**Option 2**: Scandinavian Minimalism
- **Headers**: Poppins (Bold, 700)
- **Body**: Work Sans (400)

**Option 3**: Nature-Inspired
- **Headers**: Raleway (Bold, 700)
- **Body**: Nunito (400)

#### Type Scale
```
H1: 48-56px (bold, headers)
H2: 36-42px (bold, section titles)
H3: 28-32px (semibold, subsections)
H4: 24px (semibold)
Body: 16-18px (regular)
Captions: 14px (regular)
Small text: 12px (regular)
```

### Imagery Style

#### Photography Direction
- **Primary**: High-quality landscape photography of Jämtland (forest, water, sky, seasons)
- **Facilities**: Clean, well-lit photos of cabins, camping areas, equipment
- **People**: Candid moments of families, friends, genuine joy and connection
- **Wildlife**: Moose, beavers, birds in natural habitat (not staged)
- **Canoe trips**: Action shots on water, wildlife encounters, beauty of journeys
- **Seasons**: Show spring, summer, autumn, winter variety
- **Lifestyle**: Campfires, cooking, relaxation, adventures, bonding moments

#### Photo Mood & Aesthetic
- Natural lighting preferred
- Authentic, not overproduced
- Colors: Honor the green/blue nature palette
- Composition: Rule of thirds, focus on subjects/emotion
- Processing: Warm, slightly vintage tone (not cold/clinical)
- No cheesy stock photos - real people, real moments
- Wide shots of landscape, medium shots of activities, close-ups of details

#### What NOT to Show
- Luxury resort lifestyle (mismatched brand)
- Overcrowded scenes (peace is the promise)
- Winter sports/skiing (not the offer)
- Corporate meeting rooms (family-run, not corporate)
- Sad/bored people

### Illustration & Icons

#### Icon Style
- Simplistic line-based icons
- Consistent stroke weight (1.5-2px)
- Rounded corners for warmth
- Colors from palette above
- Examples: canoe icon, tent icon, fire icon, moose icon, tree icon, water icon

#### Custom Illustrations
- Hand-drawn style elements (optional)
- Decorative nature elements (leaves, branches, water ripples)
- Map illustrations for canoe routes
- Corner accents and dividers inspired by Scandinavian patterns

## Layout & Spacing

### Spacing System (8px grid)
```
xs: 8px
sm: 12px
md: 16px
lg: 24px
xl: 32px
2xl: 48px
3xl: 64px
4xl: 96px
```

### Layout Principles
- **Whitespace is breathing room** - Don't fill every pixel
- **Asymmetrical layouts** - Mix 2-column, 3-column, full-width based on content
- **Generous padding** - Minimum 24px on mobile, 32-48px on desktop
- **Max content width**: 1200px (never feels cramped)
- **1.5-2x line height** - Easy reading, peaceful feel
- **Ample margins between sections**: 64-96px

## Component Design

### Buttons
- **Primary (CTA)**: Solid background (Sunrise Orange), white text, rounded corners (8px), min 44px height
- **Secondary**: Outlined style (Forest Green border), white background
- **Ghost**: No background, text only, underline on hover
- **Hover states**: Subtle shadow/scale, color shift, smooth transition
- **States**: Default, Hover, Active, Disabled

### Cards
- **Accommodation cards**: Image + overlay content, hover scale, shadow elevation
- **Info cards**: Border-left accent color, shadow on hover
- **Testimonial cards**: Clean, quote style, author info below
- **Subtle background**: Cream white or light gray

### Headers & Navigation
- **Sticky header**: Subtle shadow below, white background
- **Logo**: Horizontal lockup, 40-48px height
- **Navigation**: Clean, generous spacing, hover underline effect
- **Mobile menu**: Full-screen overlay or slide-in drawer
- **Active state**: Bold underline or background highlight

### Forms
- **Clean inputs**: Borders on focus, smooth transitions
- **Large labels**: Above inputs, clear hierarchy
- **Error states**: Red/orange text, icon, helpful message
- **Success states**: Green, checkmark icon
- **Input height**: 44px minimum (mobile thumb-friendly)

### Utilities
- **Shadows**: 
  - Subtle: `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
  - Medium: `0 4px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.12)`
  - Large: `0 8px 32px rgba(0,0,0,0.16)`
- **Borders**: `1px solid` in light gray or accent colors
- **Border radius**: 8px (standard), 12px (larger elements), 4px (inputs)
- **Transitions**: 200-300ms for interactive elements

## Page-Specific Design Notes

### Homepage
- **Hero**: Full viewport hero image with gradient overlay, centered headline
- **Value prop**: 3-4 key benefits with icons below hero
- **Accommodation preview**: 3 card grid (cabin/camping/canoe) with image, brief description, CTA
- **Why choose us**: Timeline or icon grid of 7 differentiators
- **Testimonial carousel**: 3-5 rotating testimonials with photos
- **Call-to-action**: Multiple "Book Now" buttons, newsletter signup

### Accommodation Pages
- **Gallery first**: Hero with high-quality images, lightbox for details
- **Detailed specs**: Clearly formatted lists (amenities, capacity, features)
- **Pricing table**: Transparent, comparison if multiple types
- **Gallery with categories**: Filter by room type
- **Reviews/testimonials**: Specific to accommodation type
- **booking CTA**: Prominent, above-the-fold and below content

### Canoe Trip Page
- **Interactive map**: Click routes for details (Hårkan, Storån, Långan)
- **Route comparison**: Table with distance, duration, difficulty, highlights
- **Photo gallery**: Action shots from canoe trips, wildlife, scenery
- **Pricing structure**: Clear tiering, family discounts highlighted
- **FAQ section**: Common canoe trip questions
- **Testimonials**: From past canoe adventurers

### About/Why Choose Us
- **Founder story**: Ove and family introduction with photo
- **Mission statement**: What Lits stands for
- **Timeline**: 30+ years of canoe center growth
- **Impact**: Number of guests served, repeat guests, animals protected
- **Values**: Community, authenticity, stewardship
- **Team photos**: Meet the people (adds warmth)

## Interactive Elements

### Hover Effects
- **Links**: Underline appears smoothly
- **Buttons**: Slight scale (1.05), color shift, shadow increase
- **Images**: Subtle zoom (1.02) or brightness shift
- **Cards**: Shadow elevation, slight lift
- All effects: 200-300ms smooth transition

### Animations
- **Page loads**: Fade in smoothly (300-400ms)
- **Scroll reveals**: Subtle fade + slide in as elements come into view
- **Image gallery**: Smooth fade between images
- **Menu**: Slide in from side or fade from top
- **No auto-animation**: Nothing moves without user interaction
- **Principle**: Beautiful but restrained, never distracting

### Micro-interactions
- Input focus: Subtle glow, cursor change
- Form submission: Loading spinner, success confirmation
- Booking calendar: Smooth date selection, availability highlighting
- Image lightbox: Smooth zoom in/out
- Scroll to top button: Fade in as user scrolls down

## Mobile Design Principles

### Mobile-First Approach
- Start design from 375px width (iPhone SE)
- Tablet layouts: 768px breakpoint
- Desktop: 1024px+

### Mobile Considerations
- **Touch targets**: 44px minimum (thumb-friendly)
- **Font sizes**: Larger (18px body minimum)
- **Images**: Full-width, optimized filesize
- **Navigation**: Hamburger menu collapsing at 768px
- **Forms**: Single column, large inputs
- **Vertical scrolling**: Multiple short sections vs. wide layouts
- **Spacing**: Generous but not wasteful (24px min padding)

## Accessibility & Inclusive Design

### WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance
- **Color contrast**: 4.5:1 for text, 3:1 for large text
- **Alt text**: Descriptive for all images
- **Keyboard navigation**: All interactive elements accessible via Tab
- **Focus indicators**: Clear, visible focus rings
- **Form labels**: Always present, not just placeholders
- **Heading hierarchy**: H1 → H2 → H3 (proper nesting)
- **Skip links**: Jump to main content

### Inclusive Features
- **Font size controls**: Optional larger text
- **Dark mode**: Nice-to-have (respects prefers-color-scheme)
- **Video captions**: If using video content
- **Readable copy**: 50-75 character line length, active voice
- **Language**: Clear, jargon-free, warm but professional

## Brand Personality in Copy

### Tone
- Warm and genuine (use "we" not corporate "Lits Camping")
- Friendly but professional
- Enthusiastic about experiences, not pushy
- Helpful and informative
- Uses Ove's personality (casual, welcoming, the smileys are his!)

### Voice Examples
✅ **Good**: "We love nothing more than greeting guests at our door and hearing their stories."  
❌ **Bad**: "Lits Camping provides premium accommodation solutions."

✅ **Good**: "Ever heard of a place where your biggest decision is whether to relax by the water or explore the wilderness?"  
❌ **Bad**: "Our strategic positioning offers optimal customer value propositions."

### Copywriting Guidelines
- Short sentences and paragraphs
- Active voice
- Benefit-focused (why guest cares, not just features)
- Personal touches (mention Ove, family, community)
- Use sensory language (peaceful, sparkling water, fresh forest air)
- Call-outs and emojis sparingly (but warmly)
